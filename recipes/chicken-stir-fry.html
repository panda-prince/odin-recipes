Chicken Stir Fry

Description

A classic, quick, and easy meal that's perfect for any occasion. Chicken stir fry is served with rice, chicken, broccoli, and your choice of stir fry suace. Whether you're in a pinch or trying to impress a date, chicken stir fry will satisfy any belly.

Ingredients

  • Oil: Neutral oil, such as canola, vegetable, or olive.
  • Chicken: Make sure they're boneless.
  • Cornstarch: Coat the chicken in cornstarch for crispy skin.
  • Onion
  • Ginger
  • Garlic
  • Salt
  • Broccoli: Or your choice of vegetables, really.
  • Water: Start the sauce with 3/4 cup of water.
  • Soy Sauce and Oyster Sauce: Essential flavors for this stir-fry.
  • Brown Sugar: Lightly sweetens the savory dish.
  • Seasonings: Garlic/Onion Powder, Black Pepper, Crushed Red Pepper.

Steps

  1. Coat the chicken in cornstarch, then cook until crisp in heated oil.
  2. Add in oinions, ginger, and garlic to cook. Transfer to plate.
  3. Cook the broccoli in the skillet.
  4. While the vegetables cook, prepare the stir-fry sauce.
  5. Add the chicken to the broccoli, and then add stir-fry sauce.
  6. Cook until sauce thickens and chicken and broccoli are fully coated.
