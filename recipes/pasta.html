Pasta

Description

Pasta is another traditional dish that can never get old. This original pasta recipe uses ground beef and red tomato sauce using your favorite noodles. Make it extra fancy with Texas Toast.

Ingredients

  • Noodles
  • Ground Beef
  • Garlic
  • Tomato Paste
  • Tomato Sauce: Regular or pre-seasoned.
  • Canned Diced Tomatoes
  • Onion
  • Oil
  • Water: 3/4th cup for red sauce.
  • Herbs: Oregano, Italian herbs and seasonings.
  • Seasonings: Garlic/Onion Powder, Paprika, Black Pepper, All-purpose seasonings.

Steps

  1. Heat oil to medium-high and saute garlic and onion to carmelize.
  2. Add ground beef and start breaking it up. Mix well with oil and garlic. Add seasonings.
  3. Cook until ground beef browns.
  4. Drain excess oil from the ground beef and pan with strainer.
  5. Place ground beef into pan. Add tomato paste, diced tomatoes, tomato paste, water, and herbs.
  6. Cover with lid and let boil. Sitr occasionally. Prepare noodles.
  7. Fill pot half-way with water or enough to fully submerge noodles.
  8. Heat over high heat. Add oil, salt, and herbs to taste.
  9. When water starts to boil, add noodles. Cover and stir occasionally over 12 minutes or until tender.
  10. Drain noodles of water with strainer.
  11. Serve in bowl with ground beef red sauce over noodles. Enjoy.
