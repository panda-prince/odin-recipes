Rice

Description

Rice can be prepared with nearly any dish. It's very versatile, nearly universal too. This recipe will teach you how to make a nice, fresh bowl of soft and flakey rice.

Ingredients

  • Rice: One cup.
  • Water: One and 1/4th cup. It is important to maintain this ratio.
  • Salt
  • Oil

Steps

  1. Heat oil in pot on high heat.
  2. When oil dances, add the rice and stir to ensure oil coats the grains. Let sit.
  3. In separate bowl, prepare water and salt to taste.
  4. Add water.
  5. Let water boil and wait until bubbles rise from within the rice heap.
  6. Cover with lid and let simmer on low heat for 10 minutes or until done. Stir occasionally.
